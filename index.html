What Is Ignite Healing AI™?

A new way to work with AI —
on your terms.

Ignite Healing AI™ is a framework for using AI as a thinking partner. It is not therapy. It is not a shortcut. It is a structured, human-led process built on consent.

It helps you stay grounded while you work. Notice what's happening in your body and mind. And turn your insights into real, usable action.

It started with one question: what if AI actually supported human wellbeing, instead of just speeding things up?

The Framework

Regulation-Centered System Design™
(RCSD)

Ignite Healing AI is built on RCSD™ — a framework that puts your nervous system at the center of how you work with technology. Most tools are built for speed and output. RCSD™ is built for people.

R
Regulation-Centered

Your nervous system comes first. Every interaction is designed to support your ability to stay calm and grounded — not push past it. Consent is built into the center of everything.

S
System

You are a system. Your patterns, your history, your body, your creativity — all connected. This framework treats you that way instead of isolating one part at a time.

D
Design

Good design is built around real human needs. RCSD applies that principle to AI — creating tools and processes that work with you, not on you.

Consent Architecture

You set
the pace.

Consent is not a checkbox. It is the structure everything else is built on.

That includes how often the system checks in with you. You set that interval — because you know your nervous system better than any tool does.

  • You set the pace — always
  • You set the limits of every conversation
  • You decide what the AI is allowed to do in any moment
  • You control how often the system checks in with you
  • You decide what is true — the AI never does
  • If a conversation doesn't increase your clarity or calm — it stops

How It Works

Track. Bring. Understand.

The patterns you notice over time become information. That information becomes insight. Insight becomes choice. And choice — eventually — becomes something you create.

01
Notice what's happening

Body signals, feelings, thought patterns. What's alive in you right now — before you try to do anything with it.

02
Bring it into the conversation

AI holds the complexity without rushing you. It slows the moment down so you can meet it differently.

03
Make better sense of yourself

Not once. Over time. Insight that doesn't stop at reflection — it becomes something you can actually use.
